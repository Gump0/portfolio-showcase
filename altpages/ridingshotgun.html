Riding Shotgun is a hectic co-op arcade game. Where one player drives and the other shoots, players must survive an onslaught of demons in the Mojave Desert to reach the sinful city of Las Vegas.



This project was created as my team's fourth-year thesis project, or "capstone game". As the group's lead programmer, I developed several of the game's essential technologies. Input management, gameplay systems, game mechanics, user interface, engine tools, graphics shaders, and more.
