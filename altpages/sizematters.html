Size Matters is a single-player puzzle expereince, where the player is challenged to cross a series of rooms to complete the game. Each room is solveable using the games primary "swap mechanic", where the player can swap positons and sizes with any of the magical objects in the room.


This project was created as a cooperative effort over the course of around three months. As one of the team's developers, I was in charge of creating the game using the Unity engine and it's built-in C# scripting framework.
